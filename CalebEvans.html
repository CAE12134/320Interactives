About Me

My name is Caleb Evans. I am a sophomore in Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism. I aspire to be a sports journalist. My dream job would be as a sports feature writer. Currently, I am a member of Northwestern's student sports radio station, WNUR Sports. With the radio station, I have had the privilege to do play-by-play and color commentary for several Northwestern basketball and baseball games.

This video showcases my journey to Northwestern University. My life started in Columbia, Missouri where I lived until I was 9. My family moved to Maryland in 2014 to be closer to my Grandparents (my mom's parents). So, we lived in Huntingtown, Maryland with them for a year until we moved 15 minutes south to Prince Frederick, Maryland.
