About Me

My name is Caleb Evans. I am a sophomore in Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism. I aspire to be a sports journalist. My dream job would be as a sports feature writer. Currently, I am a member of Northwesrn's student sports radio station, WNUR Sports. With the radio station, I have had the privilege to do play-by-play and color commentary for several Northwestern basketball and baseball games.
